# The interpreter commits: a prioritized reading, not a multivalent field

The fan-out of nine structure agents and the depth-critic explore the chart across
its full multivalent spectrum; the **interpreter** — the final synthesis — now
**commits**. It opens with the chart's centre, prioritizes the one or two things
that organize the chart, subordinates the rest, speaks about the *chart* (never the
person), and carries no orb/aspect citations. Multivalence and grounding remain
hard constraints on the *exploration* — the agents under the house rules, the
fabrication guard, and the critic — but they no longer dictate the shape of the
*output*. This **scopes ADR-0001's multivalence to the exploration phase** rather
than to the final reading.

We chose this because the instrument is *built* as a research artifact —
contestable, multivalent, grounded by visible citation — yet it is *used* as a
divination tool: an owned, prioritized, summarizable reading a practitioner brings
to a living person. On real runs the portrait came out unowned, unsummarizable,
statistically-plausible text armored with orb citations as proofs of competence.
That was mandated by the old charter ("a field of possibility, not a verdict";
"hold the tensions; do not dissolve them") — not a model failure. The two uses want
opposite final outputs, so we change only the final step and leave the exploration
intact.

## Considered options

- **Keep the multivalent-field output** (status quo). Faithful to a pure research
  framing, but the reading does not land — the project's #1 growth point. Rejected.
- **Make the structure agents commit too.** Would collapse the genuine
  inter-structure tension that *is* the instrument (ADR-0003). Rejected: commitment
  belongs only at synthesis; the agents must keep exploring.
- **Add a separate "committed summary" beside the field portrait.** Two outputs,
  doubled reading cost, and the unowned field walks straight back in. Rejected: the
  portrait itself must commit.
- **Drop grounding everywhere.** Rejected: grounding stays enforced on the
  exploration layer (house rules + fabrication guard + critic). Only the portrait's
  *voice* goes silent — the receipts sit in the layers beneath it.

## Consequences

- **ADR-0001 is scoped, not reversed.** Multivalence remains the hard constraint on
  the exploration and the chief guard against cookbook flattening; it no longer
  governs the final reading.
- **ADR-0003 is untouched.** The nine agents and their tension remain the
  instrument; only the synthesis changes.
- **ADR-0006 governs the judgement.** Quality is the analyst's Reflection, not a
  metric — no quality test is added, and the charter is expected to sharpen by
  running real charts (this is a change to the live research variable).
- **The portrait asserts about the chart, never the person** — no second-person
  claims, no advice, no direction — so the model never presumes to know a native it
  has not met; the reading is material for the practitioner.
- Matching prose updates land with this decision: the interpreter charter
  (`agents/_interpreter.md`), the interpreter step in `AGENTS.md`, and the
  *Interpreter* and *Multivalence* entries in `CONTEXT.md`.
